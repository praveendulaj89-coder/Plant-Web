We are plant babyss💚


Plants are the foundation of life on Earth. They produce oxygen, provide food, medicine, shelter, and maintain ecosystem balance. From towering trees to delicate flowers, plants enrich our planet with beauty and life.




Did you know? 💡

🌱 Plants can “talk” (just not with words)

Plants don’t have voices, but they do communicate using chemical signals. When a plant is attacked by insects, it can release chemicals into the air to warn nearby plants. Those nearby plants may then start producing natural defenses before they’re even attacked.
